# Business Logic (minimal version)

A Multi tenant application use to help business owner tracking their customer message (can be come from wa, ig or else). 

how it's work is
1. When client send message to business contact, there will be some signal send to the server to make conversion. 
2. the conversation create will display on dashboard of the company (tenant)
3. then the business owner can assign the conversation to they sales, then the sales get assign will face the customer conversation.
4. if the customer complain is not solved yet it can be come a ticket.
